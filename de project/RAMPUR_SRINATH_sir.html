I have also been actively working on this with Entelika at Incubation Centre, NIE. As part of Faculty exchange program I visited McMaster University, Canada. I was the convenor for International Symposium on Cloud Computing and Data Analytics for the year 2017. I was an Editor member of CSI adhyayan from 2016 to 2019 and also as Chairman of CSI-Mysuru Chapter (2017)
